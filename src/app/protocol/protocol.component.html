DETECTION

When you detects an issues which cause high business impact.

Either submits an incident at SOS Submit page or create "HIGH" incident in ITSM.

ALERT ON-CALL

SOS will sends incident confirmation to submitter and then start to alert Oncall.

SOS will continuing to alert Oncall every 5 minutes until Oncall acknowledge.

ACKNOWLEDGE

Oncall receives incident notification and SOS sends acknowledgement once Oncall response.

SOS then sends email to notified incident submitter that Oncall is taking care of the issue right now.

ESCALATION

If 30 minutes passed and no Oncall has acknowledge an issues, SOS automatically escalate issue to Oncall's Supervisor instead.
